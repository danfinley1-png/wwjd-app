# WWJD Catholic Dialog App - REQUIREMENTS

## 1. Catholic Authenticity Guardrails
- All AI responses must align with the **Catechism of the Catholic Church (CCC)**
- Prioritize **Magisterium**, Scripture, and Sacred Tradition
- Clearly distinguish between official teaching, pious practices, and theological opinions
- Flag any potential heresy, modernism, or doctrinal ambiguity

## 2. Core Purpose
Authentic Catholic decision-making assistant ("What Would Jesus Do?")

## 3. Non-Negotiables
- Full fidelity to the Catholic Faith
- No relativism or syncretism
- Respect for all Magisterial documents
